Physicist and RF/SDR engineer with over a decade of experience in electromagnetic simulation, antenna design, radar systems, and signal processing. Based at VENG S.A. Her work spans multi-band antenna arrays, passive bistatic radar, software-defined radio (ADRV9361), and emerging research in RF biomedical applications and precision agriculture sensing.
